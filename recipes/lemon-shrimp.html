Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup white wine
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • ~2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup pasta water
  • ~2 tbsp lemongrass paste (or juice from one lemon)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • An amount of shrimp
  • 8 oz pasta

Directions

  1. Cook pasta, reserving 1/4 of pasta water
  2. Melt 1 tbsp butter in pan, cook shrimp to desired doneness
  3. Remove shrimp, add remaining butter and saute garlic until fragrant
  4. Add wine and reduce mixture by half
  5. Add heavy cream and pasta water, let simmer for a few minutes, then add lemongrass paste
  6. Combine cornstarch with water to create a slurry, then add slurry incrementally until reaching desired consistency
  7. Toss cooked pasta in sauce, topping with shrimp
